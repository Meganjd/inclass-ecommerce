The *Tyrannosaurus* is the most **fierce** dinosaur that ever existed.

The T'rex lived in the [Late Cretaceous Period] (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous).

## Features 

- Big teeth
- Small arms
- Thick tail
